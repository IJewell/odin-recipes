Chicken Parmesan

Delicious and fast recipe to prepare chicken parmesan for anyone to enjoy!

Chicken parmesan is a very popular dish originating from Italy. The simplicity of this recipe allows for quick and easy dinner nights. This delicious dish includes chicken seasoned with a mix of breadcrumbs and topped with delicious tomato sauce and melted mozzarella cheese!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 3/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce of your choice
  • 1/4 cup of freshly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: chopped basil, grated provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees (F)
  2. Pound chicken to 1/2-inch thickness with meat mallet
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper (both sides)
  4. Use a strainer to evenly coat both sides of the chicken with flour
  5. In a separate bowl, beat eggs and set aside
  6. In another separate bowl, mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  7. Dip the seasoned chicken breasts in the beat eggs, then transfer to the breadcrumb mixture (both sides)
  8. Let chicken rest for 15 minutes
  9. Heat 1/2 olive oil in a skillet on medium heat until it begins to shimmer
  10. When oil is at desired temperature, cook chicken in oil for 2 minutes per side
  11. Transfer the cooked chicken to a baking dish and let it rest briefly
  12. Top the chicken breasts with 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce, followed by a slice of mozzarella cheese. Optional: top chicken with basil and provolone cheese
  13. Sprinkle remaining parmesan cheese on top of chicken breasts and drizzle 1/2 teaspoon of olive oil onto them
  14. Bake in the preheated oven for roughly 15-20 minutes
  15. Enjoy the deliciously prepared chicken parmesan!
