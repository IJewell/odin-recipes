Filet Mignon

One of the finest cuts of beef in the world, Filet Mignon!

This fine cut of beef comes from the tenderloin of a cow. This muscle only comprises of 1% of the entire body, leading to its expensive price. It can be prepared in a variety of ways, however this recipe is sure to satisfy the taste buds! Enjoy with guests or by yourself!

Ingredients

  • Filet Mignon (6 oz.)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Butter
  • Chopped Rosemary

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees (F)
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper (both sides)
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan with medium-high heat until it shimmers
  4. Cook filet mignon 5 minutes each side
  5. As the filet is cooking, baste with butter
  6. Transfer the skillet to an oven and cook to your liking (5 minutes for medium)
  7. Remove the pan and let rest 5 minutes before slicing
